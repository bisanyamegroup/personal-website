About Me

I am a mathematics teacher from Ghana with experience teaching at both basic and senior high school levels. I am passionate about helping students develop conceptual understanding, confidence, and problem-solving skills through clear instruction and supportive classroom practices.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe mathematics should be meaningful and accessible to all learners. I focus on conceptual understanding, real-world connections, and encouraging students to explain their reasoning. I create a classroom environment where mistakes are treated as learning opportunities.
